## Contributing

The MX specifications are developed by the community. To contribute:

1. **Report issues** at https://github.com/mx-community/specifications/issues
2. **Propose changes** via pull request
3. **Discuss** on Discord or the community forum
4. **Join a working group** for ongoing specification development

All contributions are subject to the MX Community Code of Conduct.
